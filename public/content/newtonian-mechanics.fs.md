# Gravitation - Formula Sheet

## 1. FUNDAMENTAL FORMULAS

### Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation
**F = Gm₁m₂/r²**

**Vector Form**: F₁₂ = -G(m₁m₂/r²)r̂₁₂

### Universal Gravitational Constant
**G = 6.67 × 10⁻¹¹ N·m²/kg²**

## 2. GRAVITATIONAL FIELD

### Field Intensity
**g = GM/r²**

### Field Due to Multiple Masses
**gₑ = g₁ + g₂ + g₃ + ...**

### Field Inside/Outside Sphere
- **Outside (r > R)**: g = GM/r²
- **On Surface (r = R)**: g = GM/R²
- **Inside (r < R)**: g = GMr/R³

## 3. GRAVITATIONAL POTENTIAL

### Potential at Point
**V = -GM/r**

### Potential Difference
**V₁ - V₂ = GM(1/r₂ - 1/r₁)**

### Relation with Field
**g = -dV/dr**

### Potential Energy
**U = mV = -GMm/r**

## 4. KEPLER'S LAWS

### Third Law (Period-Radius Relation)
**T² = (4π²/GM)r³**

**or** **T² ∝ r³**

### Second Law (Area Law)
**dA/dt = L/(2m) = constant**

## 5. ORBITAL MOTION

### Circular Orbital Velocity
**v₀ = √(GM/r)**

### Orbital Period
**T = 2π√(r³/GM)**

### Centripetal Force Equation
**GMm/r² = mv²/r**

### Energy in Circular Orbit
- **Kinetic Energy**: K = GMm/(2r)
- **Potential Energy**: U = -GMm/r
- **Total Energy**: E = -GMm/(2r)

### Key Energy Relations
- **K = -½U**
- **E = ½U**
- **|E| = K**

## 6. ESCAPE VELOCITY

### From Surface
**vₑ = √(2GM/R)**

### From Height h
**vₑ = √(2GM/(R+h))**

### Relation with Orbital Velocity
**vₑ = √2 × v₀**

## 7. SATELLITE MOTION

### Velocity at Height h
**v = √(GM/(R+h))**

### Period at Height h
**T = 2π√((R+h)³/GM)**

### Geostationary Satellite
- **Period**: T = 24 hours
- **Height**: h = 3.6 × 10⁷ m
- **Velocity**: v = 3.1 km/s

## 8. VARIATION OF g

### With Altitude (h << R)
**g' = g(1 - 2h/R)**

### With Altitude (Exact)
**g' = gR²/(R+h)²**

### With Depth
**g' = g(1 - d/R)**

### With Latitude
**g' = g - ω²R cos²λ**

Where:
- λ = latitude
- ω = Earth's angular velocity = 7.3 × 10⁻⁵ rad/s

## 9. PLANETARY DATA

### Earth
- **Mass**: M = 6.0 × 10²⁴ kg
- **Radius**: R = 6.4 × 10⁶ m
- **g**: 9.8 m/s²
- **Escape velocity**: 11.2 km/s

### Moon
- **Mass**: 7.4 × 10²² kg
- **Radius**: 1.74 × 10⁶ m
- **Distance from Earth**: 3.8 × 10⁸ m

### Sun
- **Mass**: 2.0 × 10³⁰ kg
- **Radius**: 7.0 × 10⁸ m
- **Distance from Earth**: 1.5 × 10¹¹ m

## 10. ELLIPTICAL ORBITS

### Kepler's Third Law
**T² = (4π²/GM)a³**

Where a = semi-major axis

### Eccentricity Relations
- **Aphelion**: rₐ = a(1 + e)
- **Perihelion**: rₚ = a(1 - e)

### Velocities in Elliptical Orbit
- **At Aphelion**: vₐ = √[GM(1-e)/(a(1+e))]
- **At Perihelion**: vₚ = √[GM(1+e)/(a(1-e))]

## 11. BINDING ENERGY

### Definition
**Binding Energy = GMm/(2r)**

### For Circular Orbit
**BE = |Total Energy| = GMm/(2r)**

## 12. TIDAL FORCES

### Tidal Force
**F_tidal ∝ Mm/r³**

### Roche Limit
**r_Roche = 2.44R(ρ_primary/ρ_satellite)^(1/3)**

## 13. WORK AND ENERGY

### Work Done in Moving Mass
**W = GMm(1/r₁ - 1/r₂)**

### Work Done Against Gravity
**W = -ΔU = GMm(1/r₂ - 1/r₁)**

### Energy to Escape from Surface
**E_escape = GMm/(2R)**

## 14. USEFUL RELATIONS

### Converting Between Parameters
- **GM = gR²** (at surface)
- **g = GM/R²**
- **R = √(GM/g)**

### Orbital Velocity vs Height
**v = √(gR²/(R+h))**

### Period vs Height
**T = 2π√((R+h)³/gR²)**

## 15. SPECIAL CASES

### At Earth's Surface
- **g = GM/R² = 9.8 m/s²**
- **v₀ = √(gR) = 7.9 km/s**
- **vₑ = √(2gR) = 11.2 km/s**

### At Great Distance (r >> R)
- **g ≈ GM/r²**
- **V ≈ -GM/r**
- **U ≈ -GMm/r**

### For Small Heights (h << R)
- **g' ≈ g(1 - 2h/R)**
- **v ≈ √(gR)(1 - h/(2R))**

## 16. DIMENSIONAL ANALYSIS

### Gravitational Constant
**[G] = M⁻¹L³T⁻²**

### Gravitational Field
**[g] = LT⁻²**

### Gravitational Potential
**[V] = L²T⁻²**

### Gravitational Potential Energy
**[U] = ML²T⁻²**

## 18. PROBLEM-SOLVING FORMULAS

### For Orbital Problems
1. **Given r, find v**: v = √(GM/r)
2. **Given v, find r**: r = GM/v²
3. **Given T, find r**: r = ∛(GMT²/4π²)
4. **Given r, find T**: T = 2π√(r³/GM)

### For Energy Problems
1. **Total energy**: E = -GMm/(2r)
2. **Kinetic energy**: K = GMm/(2r)
3. **Potential energy**: U = -GMm/r
4. **Binding energy**: BE = GMm/(2r)

### For Escape Velocity
1. **From surface**: vₑ = √(2GM/R)
2. **From height h**: vₑ = √(2GM/(R+h))
3. **Using surface gravity**: vₑ = √(2gR)

## 19. EXAM SHORTCUTS

### Quick Ratios
- **vₑ/v₀ = √2**
- **E_orbit/E_escape = 1/2**
- **K/|U| = 1/2**
- **|E|/K = 1**

### Approximations
- **For h << R**: Use (1 ± x) ≈ 1 ± x
- **For small angles**: sin θ ≈ θ, cos θ ≈ 1
- **For quick calculations**: g ≈ 10 m/s²

### Common Substitutions
- **GM = gR²**
- **v₀² = gR**
- **vₑ² = 2gR**
- **T² = 4π²r³/GM**
