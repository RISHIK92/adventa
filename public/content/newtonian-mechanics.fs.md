
# Newtonian Mechanics Formula Sheet

### Core Equations

-   **Newton's Second Law:**
    $$
    \\Sigma \\vec{F} = m \\vec{a}
    $$
-   **Momentum:**
    $$
    \\vec{p} = m \\vec{v}
    $$
-   **Impulse-Momentum Theorem:**
    $$
    \\vec{J} = \\Delta \\vec{p} = \\int \\vec{F} \\, dt
    $$

### Work and Energy

-   **Work Done by a Constant Force:**
    $$
    W = \\vec{F} \\cdot \\vec{d} = Fd \\cos(\\theta)
    $$
-   **Kinetic Energy:**
    $$
    K = \\frac{1}{2} m v^2
    $$
-   **Work-Energy Theorem:**
    $$
    W_{net} = \\Delta K = K_f - K_i
    $$
-   **Gravitational Potential Energy:**
    $$
    U_g = mgh
    $$
